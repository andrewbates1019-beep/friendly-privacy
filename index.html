Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

Friendly (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the Friendly mobile application.

---

1. Information We Collect

We collect and store information you choose to provide in the app, including:

  • Contact information (manually entered or imported from your device)
  • Notes, interactions, reminders, tags, and categories
  • App settings and preferences
  • Basic diagnostic and usage information required for app functionality
---

2. Permissions We Request

Friendly may request access to certain device features:

  • Contacts: Used to import and manage your relationships
  • Notifications: Used to send reminders you create
  • Camera: Used to scan QR codes for adding contacts

You can control or revoke these permissions at any time in your device settings.

---

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide contact management and relationship tracking features
  • Send reminders and notifications you configure
  • Generate insights about your interactions
  • Manage Free and Pro subscription entitlements
---

4. Data Storage

Your data is primarily stored locally on your device.

Some account-related data (such as authentication credentials or backup data if enabled) may be processed through secure third-party services.

---

5. Third-Party Services

Friendly may use trusted third-party services, including:

  • Apple Sign In / Google Sign-In: for authentication
  • Firebase (Google): for authentication, storage, and backend support

These services may process limited data as required to provide their functionality. We do not control their privacy practices.

---

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We do not share your personal data with third parties except when required to provide core app functionality (such as authentication or secure storage).

---

7. Analytics

Friendly does not use third-party analytics tracking by default at this time.

Analytics features may be introduced in the future to improve the app experience.

---

8. Data Retention

Your data remains stored until:

  • You delete it within the app
  • You delete your account
  • You uninstall the app
---

9. Your Rights and Choices

You have control over your data:

  • You can edit or delete your information within the app
  • You can revoke permissions at any time through your device settings
  • You may export your data if that feature is available
---

10. Children’s Privacy

Friendly is not intended for use by children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

11. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no system is completely secure.

---

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the updated date above.

---
